My Creative Statement:

My work explores the importance of friendship and company. The beauty of loving, caring and pure happiness. All the work here will consist of the little things that make

me happy when hanging out with close friends. The close bond between friends help make the tiniest details huge impacts to someone. These tiny details make a sense of comfort

which I intend to capture in my work.

These simple experiences become symbols for a group, showcasing how joy and comfort are produced. Such as the picture below, a simple game of "Spoons". But within the picture,

you can see how one person is leaned in while another person's hands are gesturing around. We can also see how everyone is sitting close and in a circle. Friendships are incredibly

important and meaningful, creating collective memories and security.
